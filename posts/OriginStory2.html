Garv was unsure about the idea at first but as we both looked into the idea further it started looking like a better and better idea. Through this process of delving deeper we found a research paper on the exact topic we were looking at linked here We thought that if two university professors had looked into the idea it would at least be promising, and so we continued the journey with this idea.
